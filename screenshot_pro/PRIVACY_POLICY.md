# Privacy Policy — ScreenshotPro

**Last updated: February 7, 2025**

## Overview

ScreenshotPro is a screenshot and annotation tool for macOS. Your privacy matters — we designed the app to work entirely on your device.

## Data Collection

**ScreenshotPro does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.**

All screenshots, annotations, settings, and history are stored locally on your Mac.

## Permissions

- **Screen Recording**: Required to capture screenshots. Images are processed and stored only on your device.
- **File Access**: Used to save screenshots to your chosen folder. Access is limited to user-selected locations.

## Third-Party Services

ScreenshotPro does not integrate with any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

## Data Storage

- Screenshots are stored in `~/Library/Application Support/ScreenshotPro/History/`
- Settings are stored in macOS UserDefaults
- All data remains on your device and is never uploaded

## Data Deletion

You can delete all stored data at any time via **Settings > Storage > Clear All History**, or by removing the app.

## Children's Privacy

ScreenshotPro does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
