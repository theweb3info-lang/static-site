FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Is my email data safe?
Absolutely. We use OAuth2 (the same secure method used by Google and Microsoft) and only access subscription-related emails. We never read personal messages, and your data is never stored on our servers or sold to third parties.
Do I need to connect my bank account?
No! Unlike Rocket Money or Trim, SubZap works by scanning your email ‚Äî no bank access required. We offer optional bank import as a premium feature for users who want it.
How does the "cancel assist" work?
For each subscription, we provide a direct cancel link (when available) or a pre-written cancellation email you can send with one tap. We also show step-by-step cancel instructions for services that require manual cancellation.
Why is it so cheap compared to alternatives?
We believe a subscription manager shouldn't be another subscription. Our costs are low (we're an indie team), so we can offer a fair one-time price. We make money from sales volume, not by taking a cut of your savings.
What email providers are supported?
We support Gmail and Outlook at launch, with more providers coming soon. You can also manually add any subscription that isn't detected automatically.
