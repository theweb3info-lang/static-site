## Description (English)

**Three tools. Zero bloat. Your PDFs, handled.**

PDF Pro is the fastest way to merge, split, and rearrange PDF pages on your Mac. No subscriptions. No cloud uploads. Just beautiful, native PDF tools that respect your privacy.

**Merge** — Drag and drop multiple PDFs, reorder them, and combine into one file. See page counts and thumbnails instantly.

**Split** — Extract a page range, split every N pages, or cherry-pick exact pages with simple syntax like `1, 3, 5-8`.

**Rearrange** — See every page as a thumbnail in a visual grid. Drag to reorder, click to select, right-click to rotate or delete. Save when you're done.

• 100% offline — your files never leave your Mac
• Native macOS app — fast, lightweight, feels like home
• Dark mode support
• Drag & drop everywhere
• Zero third-party dependencies
