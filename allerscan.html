FAQ

Questions? Answered.

How accurate is AllerScan?
AllerScan uses state-of-the-art AI to detect allergens with over 98% accuracy. It identifies allergens even when listed under scientific or chemical names. However, we always recommend double-checking with the manufacturer for severe allergies.
What allergens does it detect?
AllerScan detects all major allergens including peanuts, tree nuts, milk, eggs, wheat, soy, fish, shellfish, sesame, and more. You can customize your profile to track any specific allergen.
Does it work offline?
Basic scanning works offline using on-device AI. For the most accurate results with obscure ingredients, an internet connection is recommended for our cloud AI analysis.
What languages are supported?
AllerScan can read ingredient labels in over 20 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and more. Perfect for imported foods or traveling abroad.
Is my data private?
Absolutely. All scan processing happens on your device. We never store your food photos or health data on our servers. Your allergen profile stays on your phone and is encrypted.
