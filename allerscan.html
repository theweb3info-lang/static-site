Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How accurate is AllerScan?

AllerScan uses advanced AI trained on thousands of food labels and ingredient databases. Our detection accuracy exceeds 99% for the 14 major allergens. However, we always recommend double-checking with the manufacturer for severe allergies.

Does it work offline?

Yes! Pro users get full offline AI capability. The on-device model works without any internet connection ‚Äî perfect for travel, grocery stores with poor signal, or anywhere you need it.

What languages are supported?

AllerScan can read food labels in 25+ languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Arabic, and more. We're continuously adding new languages.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Scans are processed on-device whenever possible. We never sell your data. Your allergen profiles and scan history are encrypted and only accessible by you. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I use it for my whole family?

Yes! Pro plans include Family Sharing for up to 6 family members. Each person gets their own allergen profile, and parents can manage profiles for children.

What allergens does it detect?

The free plan covers all 14 major allergens (milk, eggs, fish, shellfish, tree nuts, peanuts, wheat, soy, sesame, and more). Pro users can add custom allergens and sensitivities.
